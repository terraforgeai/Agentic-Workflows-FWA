### Pro Tips for Invoicing:

1. **Send Promptly:** Issue the invoice same day as demo or next business day (while excitement is high)

2. **Follow Up:** 
   - Day 3: Friendly check-in ("Did you receive the invoice? Any questions?")
   - Day 7 (due date): Payment reminder if not yet received
   - Day 10: Firmer reminder + late fee warning

3. **Make Payment Easy:**
   - Offer multiple payment methods (bank, mobile money, cash)
   - Provide all necessary details clearly (account numbers, reference codes)
   - Confirm receipt immediately when payment is received

4. **Be Professional but Personal:**
   - Professional invoicing builds trust
   - Personal follow-up maintains relationship warmth
   - Balance firmness on payment terms with flexibility for genuine cashflow issues

5. **Receipt Upon Payment:**
   - Send a receipt immediately when payment is received
   - Format: "Receipt FWA-2025-001 - Payment Received: $[AMOUNT] on [Date] - Thank you!"
   - Confirms payment and reduces disputes

6. **Track Everything:**
   - Keep copies of all invoices sent
   - Track payment dates and methods
   - Document any payment arrangements or modifications
